Samurai JS  BETA

Samurai JS is a fighting game built on Phaser Javascript game framework. All images are copyrighted by SNK. It's a work in progress so please expect some funny bugs in the game.

The stories in the series take place in 18th-century Japan, during the Sakoku or seclusion period of Japan (the first four games run across 1788 and 1789) with great artistic license so that foreign-born characters (including some from places that did not exist as such in 1788) and fictional monsters can also be part of the story. The plot of each game is quite different, but they circle a central group of characters and a region in Japan. For more information on how to play this game, don't forget to follow us on our social media pages below.

    If using a keyboard, the keyboard layout is below:

  • A: Walk backward
  • S: Crouch
  • D: Walk forward
  • W: Jump
  • I: Medium slash
  • O: Medium kick
  • P: Heavy slash

    • The movement buttons (e.g. ASDW) acts like a joystick. To do a quarter circle forward motion, press S > D. To do an uppercut motion, press D > S > D. To block an attack, press the button away from the opponent's direction. To perform a critical art, press E twice.
